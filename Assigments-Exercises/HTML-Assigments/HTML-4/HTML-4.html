Top 3 Things I Need for a Movie Marathon

  • A comfy couch or bed
  • Snacks and drinks
  • A Netflix or a list of movies

Steps to Get Started

  1. Pick a theme or list of movies to watch
  2. Set up the watching area with comfort in mind
  3. Prepare snacks and drinks to enjoy during the marathon
  4. Hit play and immerse myself in the stories!
